# Off-Page SEO Playbook (Israel)

## Goals
- Increase authority signals for PR, strategic communications, AI lectures and conference moderation.
- Earn high-trust Israeli links and mentions (not paid spam links).

## Priority Outreach Targets
- Israeli business media and economic publications.
- Technology podcasts and founder communities.
- Conference organizers and event landing pages.
- Universities, executive programs and public-sector professional networks.

## Asset Types to Pitch
- Expert commentary on media strategy and AI communication.
- Guest op-eds on crisis communication and public trust.
- Conference recap insights and keynote clips.
- Case-study summaries with measurable outcomes.

## Link Quality Rules
- Prefer editorial placements on real sites with human readership.
- Anchor text should be natural and mixed (brand + topical).
- Avoid low-quality link farms and PBNs.

## 90-Day Execution Cadence
1. Weeks 1-2: build target list (50 sites) and contact map.
2. Weeks 3-6: publish 4 expert pieces + 4 interviews/podcast mentions.
3. Weeks 7-10: distribute 3 case-study stories and event collaborations.
4. Weeks 11-12: audit link profile, disavow obvious spam, iterate.

## Reporting
- New referring domains (monthly).
- Topical relevance of new links.
- Assisted conversions from referral and organic branded traffic.
