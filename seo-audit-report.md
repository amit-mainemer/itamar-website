# SEO Audit Report (Israel Market)

## Baseline Status
- Site structure: single-page Hebrew website in `index.html`.
- Technical SEO: missing canonical URL, hreflang, robots meta strategy, and structured JSON-LD.
- Indexing assets: `robots.txt` and `sitemap.xml` are not present in project root.
- International targeting: no dedicated English entry point for Israeli English-language searches.
- Content architecture: no cluster landing pages for high-intent services (PR, strategy, AI lectures, conference moderation).
- E-E-A-T trust assets: no dedicated privacy policy, terms, or case studies pages.
- Measurement layer: no documented KPI dashboard or SEO experimentation workflow.

## Priority Gaps
1. Technical discoverability and entity signals (meta + schema + sitemap + robots).
2. Search-intent coverage with dedicated service pages.
3. Trust and authority pages to improve E-E-A-T.
4. Measurement and iteration framework for ranking and lead growth.

## Recommended Rollout
- Phase 1: technical SEO foundation.
- Phase 2: keyword cluster content pages (Hebrew + English).
- Phase 3: authority and trust reinforcement pages.
- Phase 4: off-page outreach playbook for Israeli media/PR ecosystem.
- Phase 5: KPI tracking and monthly SEO/AEO experiments.
