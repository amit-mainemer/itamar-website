# SEO KPI Dashboard (Israel Market)

## Business KPIs
- Monthly organic leads (consulting inquiries)
- Monthly lecture booking inquiries
- Organic conversion rate (lead form + click-to-call)

## Visibility KPIs
- Top-3 rankings for priority Hebrew terms
- Top-10 rankings for Israeli English terms
- Organic CTR by landing page
- Impressions and clicks by cluster page

## Technical KPIs
- Core Web Vitals: LCP, INP, CLS
- Indexed pages count
- Crawl errors (4xx/5xx)
- Mobile usability issues

## AI Search KPIs
- Share of branded + non-branded AI referral sessions
- FAQ impressions and rich result appearances
- Citation rate of brand/service pages in AI answers (manual sample)

## Review Cadence
- Weekly: traffic, leads, crawl/indexing checks
- Monthly: rankings, CTR optimization and content updates
- Quarterly: strategy reset and new cluster expansion
